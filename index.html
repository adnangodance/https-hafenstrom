HAFEN is an SME located in Norway, specifically north of the Arctic Circle. Established in 2013, this is a project-oriented company focusing on research and innovation actions

Hafenstrom AS was established in 2013 as an ESCO service company for the production of new renewable power and energy efficiency aimed at shore power plants for ports in Norway.
The company became involved early on in European research and development work within IoT and digitization platforms, e.g., for smart neighbourhoods (VICINITY-H2020), energy flexibility for ports (GIFT-H2020), open digital ecosystem for smart societies and rural areas (AURORAL-H2020).
We currently have 6 employees and are based in Narvik. Hafenstrom leads the international the standardization committee for Internet-of-Things and Digital Twins under the auspices of the Norwegian Electrotechnical Committee (NEK).
